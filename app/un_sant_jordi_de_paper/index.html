Description

Paper Knight is a 2.5D fantasy platformer set in a beautiful world of crafts, which will tell the legend of Sant Jordi! Make your way with a sword, jump over obstacles and overcome all levels and enemies to save the kingdom from the evils that hide it in the shadows!

Features

  • Enjoy a hand-made and beautiful paper aesthetic.
  • View the legends of Catalonia from a new perspective.
  • Run through and complete hand-crafted levels and various challenges.
  • 6 hours of adventure, twists and turns.
